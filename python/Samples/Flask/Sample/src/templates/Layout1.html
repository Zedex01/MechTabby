Basic Glitches

These are the basic glitches most important to any speedrunner of oot and cover all of the most versatile ones.

Infinite Sword Glitch (ISG)

ISG is largely the simpliest and easiest glitch in all of oot. You simply have to intterupt a sword slash while it is happening by either speaking to someone or picking something up. The most common uses are with navi on enemies and bombs.
